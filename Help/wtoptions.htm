Changing Options Walkthrough

From the main menu, choose File, Options (Ctrl-T)

  1. Choose Sign Language
  2. Choose the Gloss language.
  3. If using more than one gloss language per sign language, check the Bilingual Gloss and choose the second gloss language.

Tips

These settings affects the Dictionary, SignLists modules and the Gloss to Sign function.

The Dictionary will only show the entries under the current sign language.  Choose the sign language appropriately.
