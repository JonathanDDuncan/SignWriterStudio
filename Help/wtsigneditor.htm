SignEditor Walkthrough

Open the SignEditor from the Dictionary or a Document

  1. Select a symbol from the Favorites, All or Search tabs by clicking on it.
  2. A chooser will display to help make choose the actual symbol to add. (Different chooser displayed depending on the type of symbol)
  3. Drag it from the box to the sign area.
  4. Right click on sign area for more options.
  5. Click on Accept when you have finished
