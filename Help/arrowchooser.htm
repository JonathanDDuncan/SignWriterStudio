Arrow Chooser™

The Arrow Chooser™ helps choose the oritentation of arrows. 

The chooser distinguishes between several types of arrows depending on the type of arrow chosen.  Here are the possibilities: "Right Hand, Left Hand, Superposed Hands, Right Hand Flipped, Left Hand Flipped, Superposed HandsFlipped, No Arrowhead".

The symbol in the top right hand corner is the currently selected symbol.

Tips

All of the settings can be selected using the keyboard.

You can switch from the wall plane to the floor plane and back using number 5 key. The top circle of rotations is for the wall plane and the lower circle of rotations is for the floor plane.

The numbers of the hand rotations selections are the 8 outside numeric keys of the numeric keyboard.  Each of the 8 pointing away from the center.

Being the case that an arrow does not have a corresponding arrow in the other plane other circle of rotations does not show.

If a group of arrows isn't in the wall plane or the floor plane, the picture of the arrows in the plane will not display.  The controls will provide an approximate orientation for the arrows.

The flip checkbox is to flip certain arrows.  Click on the checkbox or press the '-' to flip an arrow.

Keyboard

General keys
0 Switch between arrow types.
5 Switch between wall plan and floor plane selection.
8 North of the plane. Pointing straight up in the wall plane or strait ahead in the floor plane.
9 North-East of the plane. Pointing upward-right diagonal in the wall plane or forward-right diagonal in the floor plane.
6 East of the plane. Right in both planes.
3 South-East of the plane. Pointing downward-right diagonal in the wall plane or backward-right diagonal in the floor plane.
2 South of the plane. Straight down in the wall plane or strait back in the floor plane.
1 South-West of the plane. Pointing downward-left diagonal in the wall plane or backward-left diagonal in the floor plane.
4 West of the plane. Left in both planes.
7 North-West of the plane. Pointing upward-left diagonal in the wall plane or forward-left diagonal in the floor plane.
- Flip the arrow
Alt-C Change the selected symbol to the currently choosen one.
Alt-F Find the group of the selected symbol in All Symbols
Enter Move over to the sign area and adds the selected symbol to the sign.
Escape Move over to the Favorite Symbols area.

Mousing

  • Choose the drop down box to choose between arrow types.
  • Click on the checkbox to flip an arrow.
