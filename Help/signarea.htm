Sign Area

This area is where the symbols are put together to create a sign.

Tips

When one or more symbols are selected, they turn purple and the other symbols become black.

If only one symbol is selected and then move back to a chooser, the chooser will change the selected symbol in the sign.

The numbers move the selected symbols to a 9 space grid corresponding to the numeric keypad.

Right click the sign for the popup menu to see available options.

If you select one symbol in the sign, the chooser finds the symbol in case you want to modify it.  Just hit Escape and select the appropriate symbol.

If two symbols are partialy overlaping and you only want to select one, click on part of the symbol that does not overlap the other symbol.

If two symbols are overlaping and you only want to select one, hold down the Ctrl key while clicking on the selected symbols.

Position the mouse cursor over a symbol for a short description.

If you make a mistake hit Ctrl-Z to undo you last change.

Keyboard

General keys
Arrow keys Moves the selected symbols 1 pixels.
Shift-Arrow keys Moves the selected symbols 15 pixel.
Ctrl-Up Move the symbols closer to the front
Ctrl-Down Move the symbols further to the back
7 Moves selected symbols to the Top Left of the sign area.
8 Moves selected symbols to the Top  of the sign area.
9 Moves selected symbols to the Top Right of the sign area.
4 Moves selected symbols to the Left of the sign area.
5 Moves selected symbols to the Middle of the sign area.
6 Moves selected symbols to the Right of the sign area.
1 Moves selected symbols to the Bottom Left of the sign area.
2 Moves selected symbols to the Bottom of the sign area.
3 Moves selected symbols to the Bottom Right of the sign area.
Insert Insert the selected symbol from the chooser into the sign.
Delete Delete the selected symbols.
Ctrl-Delete Clear the sign.
Ctrl-A Select All
Alt-N Select next symbol
Alt-Ctrl-N Select previous symbol.
Alt-Shift-N Select next symbol and maintain already selected symbols
Alt-Shift-Ctrl-N Select previous symbol and maintain already selected symbols
Alt-C Center selected symbols in sign area.
Alt-Shift-C Center head of selected symbols in sign area.
Ctrl-Shift-C Copy sign
Ctrl-C Copy sign image.
Alt-D Duplicate selected symbols.
Alt-H Change hand color of selected symbols.
Alt-P Change palm color of selected symbols.
Alt-B Change background color of sign.
Alt-O Overlap selected symbols.
Alt-S Seperate Symbols
Alt-V Paste sign
Alt-Z Undo
Alt-Y Redo
Alt-P Change principal color
Alt-S Change secondary color
Alt-B Change background color
Ctrl-Alt-C Colorize
Ctrl-Alt-B Change to Black and white
+ Increase size of selected symbols
- Decrease size of selected symbols
Enter If any symbols are selected, unselect them.
If no symbols are seleted, one is selected.
Escape Move to the chooser area.

Mousing

  • Click on a symbol to select it.
  • Hold the Ctrl key while clicking to select additional symbols.
  • Drag selected symbols to another part of the sign.
