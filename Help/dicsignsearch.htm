Dictionary Sign Search

The dictionary sign search is to search the signs in a Dictionary by symbol.  Write a few symbols that you want to find in the dictionary and click accept.  All the signs in the Dictionary that have those symbols in them will appear in the grid.

Tips

The search will also list signs with symbols that are different but have symbols with the same meaning.
