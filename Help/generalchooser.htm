General Chooser

The general chooser is an icon list of sub symbols of a group or sub group when an hand chooser or arrow chooser cannot be used.

Tips

Use the numbers to move around the icon list quickly.

Use the Find feature to find a similar simbol to the one selected in the All Symbols icon list.

Keyboard

General keys
Left arrow When positioned on a category in the icon list, colapses it.
Right arrow When positioned on a category in the icon list, expands it.
Up arrow When in the icon list, move to the preceding icon symbol.
Down arrow When in the icon list, move to the next icon symbol.
1 Displays first subgroup of the icon list.
2 Displays second subgroup of the icon list.
3 Displays third subgroup of the icon list.
4 Displays fourth subgroup of the icon list.
5 Displays fifth subgroup of the icon list.
6 Displays sixth subgroup of the icon list.
Alt-C Change the selected symbol to the currently choosen one.
Alt-F Find the group of the selected symbol in All Symbols
Enter Move over to the sign area and adds the selected symbol to the sign.
Escape Move over to the Favorite Symbols area.

Mousing

Except for the group and subgroup icons, all icons can be dragged directly to the Sign area.

  • Click on the '+' in the icon list to expand the icons in a category and the '-' to colapse a category.
  • Double click on a category, group or subgroup to expand or colapse it.
  • Click on a symbol to select it.
  • Drag a symbol to the Sign area to include it in the sign.
  • Drag the selected symbol from the top right corner to the Sign area.
