Gloss to Sign Walkthrough

  1. Open a document.
  2. From the menu  Signs, Gloss to Sign (Ctrl-G)
  3. Enter the signs you want seperated by spaces.
  4. Click on the Gloss to Sign Button.  For each sign a list of matching signs appear.
  5. Put a check mark beside the ones you want.
  6. Click on the Accept button when you are satisfied with your results.

Tips

The sign language in options with the primary gloss language will be searched.  Secondary gloss language and other sign languages will not appear in the results.

You can modify a search term my right clicking on a list of signs to change or remove it without loosing your other selections.
